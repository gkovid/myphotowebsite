Dreamer. Engineer. Artist.

I am Kovid Gurrala. I graduated from Virginia Tech with a B.S. in Computer Science and a minor in Theatre Arts. I have an eye for photography and explore my city through the lense of the camera. I am passionate about the latest technologies in the market and how they can be used to better the world around me.

I had the fortune to work in a variety of companies and cultures that helped to broaden my view of the world and the computing industry. I have worked at Zoomdata, Virginia Bioinformatics Institute, NEXTStep E-Commerce, Visa Inc., Civil Engineerig Network Dynamics Laboratory and am currently working at Microsoft.

In my spare time, I like to experiment with new and exciting technologies; and, travel to new locations to marvel at the sights through the lense of a camera.
